Library

Our Learning Resource

Our library is enriched with vast collection of books, documents, booklets, newspapers, audio-visual CDs & DVDs, Industrial reports and library also subscribed no of magazines and journals in different subjects which are very essential for our commerce and management students. During the academic year students can issue maximum two books from library. We organize library orientation programme, Book Exhibition as well as Book review competition for enhancing knowledge of students. Our library is well furnished and computerized with barcode system.
