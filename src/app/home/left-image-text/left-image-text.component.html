History
Shri Dadabhai Naoroji Institute of Business Administration (DNIBA) is managed by Charotar Education Society, Anand and affiliated to Sardar Patel University, Vallabh Vidyanagar.

"Education is the great enabler that allows individual to unlock their potential”. Literacy skills are more critical today than ever before, and they will become even more vital in future.
It has emerged from Shri V.Z. Patel Commerce College and now separated from it. Shri V. Z. Patel Commerce College located at Anand, was established in the year 1997. It is affiliated to Sardar Patel University, Vallabh Vidyanagar. The College has started its first course of B.Com. in the same year 1997 with the enrollment of 98 students. The success of B.Com inspired us to carry forward the torch of enlightening the students and offering them a chance to study for the degree of Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA – General) which was started in the year 2005 with the enrollment of 72 students. It was a great honour for the college to achieve 100 percent result in Sardar Patel University through the first batch of BBA Students in the year 2008-2009.

It is indeed a matter of great honour and pride for us to introduce a new revolutionary course BBA – ITM (Honours) in 2008. The college has developed a mechanism for Quality Assurance. Innovative practices are adopted for an all round development for our students.
In academic year 2009-10 college is accredited with ‘B’ Grade by NAAC. From the Academic year 2010-2011 it has separated its courses of BBA (Gen.) and BBA –ITM (Honours) under the title of Shri D. N. Institute of Business Administration.
In academic year 2011-12 Shri D.N. Institute of Business Administration has also been certified by ISO 9001:2008.
The Institution is named on the Great personality of India – Shri Dadabhai Naoroji, who played an active role in the freedom movement of the country.
The motto of the Institute is – Foundation for Excellence which signifies development of students’ personality in each and every respect.
Young, energetic and dedicated Faculties motivate and inspire students to expand their horizons by giving them fruitful knowledge about life and career. The academic activity is continuously geared up and monitored to cope with emerging trends of Education. The faculty members are not only providing Quality Education, but also Enriching and Excelling in imparting Professional Education which is reflected in their spirit and contribution at many National and International Forums.
